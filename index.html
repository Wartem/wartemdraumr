ANCIENT NORSE — A UNIFIED IDENTITY

A unified identity. Two expressions of the same ancient current.

Wartem Draumr is the artistic identity through which an ancient spirit is restored as lived presence and function. Working from an Ancient Norse foundation, the music unfolds through two distinct sonic paths. Primal Norse is visceral, bodily, and ritual-driven. Norse Folk is narrative, textural, and rooted in place and memory. Both are inseparable expressions of the same underlying force.
